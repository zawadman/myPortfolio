About Me

Hi, I'm Zawad Ul Mutawassit, a recent graduate in Computer Science (specializing in Visual Computing & Games) with a minor in Mathematics from Memorial University of Newfoundland. My passion lies in creating immersive interactive experiences, optimizing performance, and crafting intelligent systems. With expertise in C++, JavaScript, React.js, Three.js, and Unreal Engine 5, I've developed diverse projects ranging from real-time 3D simulations and machine learning-driven applications to intuitive UI/UX designs and dynamic web apps. My work often integrates advanced algorithms, computer vision, and AI techniques, exemplified by projects involving ResNet-50 CNNs for feature extraction and reinforcement learning for adaptive game AI. I enjoy tackling challenging problems through clean, efficient code and thrive in collaborative, dynamic environments. When not coding, I mentor students in programming, explore new technologies, and actively contribute to open-source initiatives. I'm always eager to connect, collaborate, and contribute to innovative projects—feel free to reach out!
